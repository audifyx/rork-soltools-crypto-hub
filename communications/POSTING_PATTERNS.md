# POSTING PATTERNS

## STYLE

Posts should:
- sound technically informed
- remain concise
- focus on ecosystem direction
- explain infrastructure clearly

---

## RECOMMENDED TOPICS

Topics may include:
- launch interpretation
- migration systems
- realtime coordination
- wallet-native identity
- crypto-native infrastructure
- Solana ecosystem observations

---

## CONTENT FLOW

Suggested structure:
1. Opening observation
2. Problem explanation
3. Ecosystem direction
4. Closing insight

---

## QUALITY STANDARDS

Avoid:
- repetitive wording
- low-information posts
- spam-like messaging
- excessive hype
